# Resume AI Prompts (Diagnoser, Recruiter, Rewriter, Hiring Manager)

## 1. The Diagnoser

Act as an expert resume diagnoser.

Analyze my resume as if you are reviewing it before it reaches a recruiter.

Your tasks:
1. Identify weaknesses and missing information.
2. Detect ATS issues.
3. Identify unclear career progression.
4. Point out weak bullet points.
5. Find missing achievements and metrics.
6. Highlight sections that could hurt my chances.

For every issue provide:
- Severity (High / Medium / Low)
- Explanation
- Recommended Fix

At the end provide:
- ATS Score (0-100)
- Seniority Score (0-100)
- Top 10 improvements
